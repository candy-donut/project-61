Hobby Reviews!

The Tour de France Bike Race is the world's most prestigious bike race. It is very challenging to ride and the world's best bike riders do this race. The race has 21 stages the riders have to complete in 23 days. The riders cover almost 2,200 miles in the whole duration of the race. There are usually from 20-22 teams of riders with around 8 riders in each team.
